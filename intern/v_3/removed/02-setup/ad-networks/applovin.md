# AppLovin

This guide instructs you on how to create ad units for iOS and Android on AppLovin website.

## Step 1: Create an AppLovin Account

1. Visit the [AppLovin sign up page](https://www.applovin.com/signup).
2. Fill out the sign-up form with your details like Name, Company Name, Email, etc.
3. Review the terms of service, then click 'Sign Up'.

## Step 2: Set Up Your Apps

1. Log-in into your newly created account.
2. On your dashboard, click on '+ App' button to add your app.
3. Define whether your app is live in any App Store by selecting 'Yes' or 'No'.
4. If 'Yes' then you need to fetch the details from the store; if 'No' then fill-up the form manually.
5. Select the platform (iOS or Android) for your app and click 'Add App'.

## Step 3: Create Ad Units

### For iOS

1. Go to 'Monetize' > 'Ad setup for iOS'.
2. Click '+ Zone' for new ad unit.
3. Set the zone type and ad format (Interstitial, Banner, Rewarded).
4. Fill up other details like Zone ID, Currency, Conversion rate, etc.
5. Click 'Save' to generate the iOS SDK key for the ad unit.

### For Android

1. Go to 'Monetize' > 'Ad setup for Android'.
2. Click '+ Zone' for new ad unit.
3. Set the zone type and ad format (Interstitial, Banner, Rewarded).
4. Fill up other details like Zone ID, Currency, Conversion rate etc.
5. Click 'Save' to generate the Android SDK key for the ad unit.

***

You've successfully created your AppLovin account and set up ad units for both iOS and Android apps! Go ahead and integrate these ad units in Ads Kit under AdsKitSettings -> Services -> AppLovin-> Ad Meta Array.
